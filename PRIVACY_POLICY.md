# Privacy Policy for Kuri Draw

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

The **Mukkilapedia Team** ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how **Kuri Draw** (the "App") handles your information. 

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
**Kuri Draw is a fully offline application.** 
- **Local Storage Only:** We do not use any external servers, databases, or cloud services to store your data. All data you enter into the App (including names, draw names, payment statuses, and winner histories) is stored entirely locally on your device.
- **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any personal information or usage analytics to third-party services.

## 2. Permissions & Device Features
To provide its core functionality, the App may request access to certain native device features:

- **Microphone & Screen Recording (Media Projection):** 
  The App includes a "Genuine Mode" that records the screen while the lucky draw wheel spins. This requires permission to capture the screen and record audio. 
- **Photo Library / Storage Access:** 
  The recorded videos are saved directly to your device’s local gallery/storage. You then have the option to manually share these videos using your device's native sharing capabilities.

**Important:** The screen recordings and audio captured by the App never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them (for example, via WhatsApp, Email, or Social Media). We have no access to your recordings, photos, or files.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics (such as Google Analytics), or advertising SDKs. The App operates independently without requiring an internet connection.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13, as we do not collect any data at all.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
